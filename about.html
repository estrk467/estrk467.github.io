About Me

Hi! I'm Karla. I am a recent graduate from California State University, East Bay. I majored in Computer Science and I am currently in the process of looking for a full time job. I'm a first-generation student and I could not be more proud of myself for being able to complete my degree this Spring. It was tough journey but I am glad I was able to commit to it. I learned a lot during my years in school and I am excited to get to use my leadership and technological skills.

What attracted me to Computer Sciece was the fact that there was going to be a lot of math. I didn't really know what I wanted to declare my major when I was in high school but I was lucky enough to get a lot of advice from my high school Calculus professor. I would of never in a milllion years thought that I was going to be majoring in computer science. I didn't even know what computer science was when my professor mentioned it. LOL. Long story short, I did not know what I was getting myself into. Luckily, my stubborness and determination is what helped me get my degree. I am now able to use those qualities when solving complicated problems.
